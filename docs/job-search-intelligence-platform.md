# Job Search Intelligence Platform

## Overview

The Job Search Intelligence Platform is an AI-assisted workflow system used to organize, review, prioritize, and track employment opportunities.

The system supports career operations by reducing scattered manual tracking and creating a structured pipeline for job discovery, resume targeting, recruiter follow-up, and interview preparation.

## Business Problem

Job searching often becomes disorganized across job boards, emails, spreadsheets, resumes, recruiter messages, and follow-up reminders.

Common issues include:

- Duplicate job postings
- Missed follow-ups
- Unclear application status
- Poor visibility into interview pipeline
- Difficulty matching resumes to role requirements
- Time lost reviewing low-fit opportunities

## Solution

This workflow centralizes career activity into a repeatable operating system.

## Core Capabilities

- Job opportunity tracking
- Application status tracking
- Recruiter contact tracking
- Interview tracking
- Follow-up planning
- Resume targeting notes
- Role-fit review
- Daily or weekly career summaries

## Workflow

```text
Job Lead Found
      ↓
Role Reviewed
      ↓
Fit and Requirements Checked
      ↓
Resume Version Selected
      ↓
Application Submitted
      ↓
Follow-Up Scheduled
      ↓
Interview / Rejection / Archive
```

## AI-Assisted Functions

- Summarizing role requirements
- Extracting key skills from job descriptions
- Comparing resume language to job postings
- Drafting follow-up messages
- Preparing interview talking points
- Identifying skill gaps
